life is good

I live with my wife and our two sons just north of Chicago. We're near to friends and family; we have easy-access to beaches, trails, parks, and playgrounds.

Before having kids I bounced from place to place coaching women's soccer for a number of collegiate and professional teams. It was a fantastic experience filled with friendships and fun.

I enjoyed the big cities (Miami, Chicago, Boston, NYC), but life is good here in the burbs, and I'm not going anywhere.

i like building stuff

I work for ServiceNow, an enterprise software company specializing in workflow optimization.

My team is creating a suite of modern UIs for both internal users and SN's clients, allowing them to quickly create and configure experiences in the NOW platform.

In my early coding years I conceptualized, coded, and supported one of the first-to-market SaaS programs for collegiate athletics: TEAMDesktop. In my spare time I mess around with frameworks and interesting APIs.
